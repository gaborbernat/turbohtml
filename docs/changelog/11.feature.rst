Escape untrusted text for HTML with :mod:`turbohtml.migration.markupsafe`, a drop-in for `markupsafe
<https://markupsafe.palletsprojects.com/>`__'s public surface (:class:`turbohtml.migration.markupsafe.Markup`,
:func:`turbohtml.migration.markupsafe.escape`, :func:`turbohtml.migration.markupsafe.escape_silent`,
:func:`turbohtml.migration.markupsafe.soft_str`, :class:`turbohtml.migration.markupsafe.EscapeFormatter`). A `Jinja2
<https://jinja.palletsprojects.com/>`__, WTForms, or Werkzeug project migrates by changing the import, and ``escape``
runs two to four times faster on the small strings autoescaping interpolates.
